AI CHAT ACTIVITY STRUCTURE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Each AI Chat activity MUST contain:

A. Conversation Flow (mandatory states)
1. Warm greeting
2. Personal connection
3. Guided practice
4. Free but controlled practice
5. Gentle correction when needed
6. End-of-session recap

B. Prompt Set
Create a promptSet array with 5–8 steps.
Each step should:
• Be written as an instruction to the AI
• Use simple English
• Reflect real conversation, not exercises
• Match the lesson grammar strictly

Example format:
- "Greet the student and ask their name."
- "Ask where they are from and encourage a full sentence."
- "Correct only ONE mistake if needed and praise them."

C. End-of-session recap logic
At the end of each lesson, the AI MUST:
• Say what the student did well
• Mention 1 thing to review (max)
• Encourage them to continue
