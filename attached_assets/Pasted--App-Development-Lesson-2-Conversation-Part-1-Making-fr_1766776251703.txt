Part 4 - Role Play - Shopping


The AI should help students act out this scenario of buying things for English Class
1.) You need to buy (usted necesita comprar):
• El lápiz ($0.10) • El libro ($9)


AI: Hi! How are you?
Student: I am fine, thank you. How much does the pencil cost?


AI: The pencil costs ten cents.
Student: Ok. How much does the book cost?


AI: The book costs nine dollars.
Student: I will take the pencil and the book.


AI: Thanks! Bye.
Student: See you!
2.) You need to buy
• El bolígrafo ($1) • El paquete de papel ($2)
AI: Hi! How are you?
Student: I am fine, thank you. How much does the pen cost?


AI: The pen costs one dollar.
Student: Ok. How much does the pack of paper cost?


AI: The pack of paper costs two dollars.
Student: I will take the pen and the pack of paper.


AI: Thanks! Bye.
Student: See you!
3.) You need to buy
• El marcador ($3) • La pizarra ($10)
AI: Hi! How are you?
Student: I am fine, thank you. How much does the marker cost?


AI: The marker costs three dollars.
Student: Ok. How much does the whiteboard cost?


AI: The whiteboard costs ten dollars.

AI: Thanks! Bye.

Part 5 - Relevance


The conversation partner should help the student identify 10 highly relevant words and explain the following:
Cuando estás aprendiendo hablar otro idioma ¡tiene que hacerlo relevante!


¿A qué te dedicas? What do you do for work?


Based on what the student says, the AI should come up with the 10 most frequently used nouns according to the person’s job


Then ask:


¿Qué haces para divertirte? What do you do for fun?


Based on what the student says, the AI should come up with the 10 most frequently used nouns according to what they do for fun.


IMPORTANT - This should be a new feature that unlocks, so students can personalize their learning experience anytime they want!
